# StriSakhi — Three Companions, Always By Your Side

**StriSakhi** is a voice-first AI assistant helping rural Indian women access their legal rights, health information, and government benefits — in their own language, offline, for free.

---

## The Problem

A woman in a village in Uttar Pradesh is being beaten by her husband. She doesn't know she has a legal right to stay in her home. She doesn't know she can get free legal help. She can't read complex legal documents. And she's scared to ask for help.

**68% of Indian women have faced domestic violence. Most don't know their rights.**

---

## What StriSakhi Does

Three AI companions, each specialized:

### ⚖️ Kanoon Sakhi (Legal Companion)
Explains legal rights in simple language. Knows the Domestic Violence Act, Property Rights, Workplace Harassment laws. Gives 3 concrete steps to take today. Connects to free legal aid (NALSA: 15100).

### 🌿 Sehat Sakhi (Health Companion)
Answers health questions — pregnancy, child illness, mental health. Guides to government health schemes. Always recommends seeing a doctor for serious symptoms.

### 📜 Yojana Sakhi (Scheme Companion)
Finds government schemes the woman qualifies for — housing, health insurance, financial assistance, education. Tells her exactly where to apply.

---

## How It Works

1. **Speak or type** in Hindi, English, or Bengali
2. The AI asks 2-3 simple questions to understand the situation
3. Gets relevant legal/health advice backed by real documents
4. Press 🔊 to hear the response in a warm Indian female voice
5. All offline — no internet required after setup

---

## Voice First Design

Designed for women with low literacy:
- Large tap targets
- 🔊 Listen button to hear every response
- Hindi female voice (Piper priyamvada) — warm, natural Indian accent
- Slow speech rate (20% slower than normal — easier to understand)
- One question at a time — never overwhelming

---

## Privacy First

- **No data sent to any cloud** — everything runs on your machine
- **No account required**
- **No ads, no tracking**
- Voice recordings are never stored — transcribed and discarded immediately

---

## Technology

Built with:
- **Gemma 4 E2B** — Google's multilingual model for text and voice understanding
- **Piper TTS** — offline Indian female voice (61MB)
- **Real legal documents** — DV Act 2005, Hindu Succession Act, POSH Act 2013
- **Fully offline** — runs without internet after initial setup
